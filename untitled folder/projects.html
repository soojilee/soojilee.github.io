Speak Out. Express yourself through your voice!

Record your voice, share, and connect with people. You can upload your recordings to your channel, explore to find other people's channel to follow. You can see the latest newsfeed of people you are following from the "Home" tab. There are still more features to come!

Stop Procrastinating. Keep track of daily activities for better productivity.

You can create pages to represent a category of activities they wish time-manage. For example, if you create a page called "Work Out," then you can create a row named "Yoga". You can keep track of the time of all yoga sessions using a stop watch. The stop watch keeps running even if you turn off the app until you come back to stop the session.

How good is your sensory memory? Test yourself.

You are presented with a random color for one second. Then you are presented with three colors, from which you have to identify the color they previously saw. If you take too long to answer, game times out and ends; if you choose the wrong color then game ends. You can share the game on facebook after you are done playing.

Navigate the Campus with Ease. UW Campus Guide.

Main idea of the application was to overlay a view with the name of the building on Camera view so that you can identify the building they are looking at. Clicking on the name of the building will give some info about the building. You can also find routes between buildings for fast navigation.
